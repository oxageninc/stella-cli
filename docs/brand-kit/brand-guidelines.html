03 — Typography

Wordmark

The wordmark is set in DejaVu Sans Mono, all caps, drawn as outlines so it renders identically everywhere with no font dependency. The monospace rhythm mirrors the geometric mark.

typeface
DejaVu Sans Mono · Regular · uppercase
letter-spacing
0.30 em (standalone & horizontal) · 0.22 em (vertical lockup)
case
Always set “STELLA” in caps. Never mixed-case, italic, or condensed.
min size
Cap height ≥ 12 px in the wordmark; below that, use the mark alone.
Swapping fonts: the wordmark is delivered as vector outlines. To rebrand onto a licensed monospace (e.g. Space Mono, DM Mono, JetBrains Mono), drop the .ttf into the pipeline and every lockup regenerates from the same metrics.

08 — Usage

Do & don’t

Do

  • Keep the clear space (X = ½ icon) on every side.
  • Use the gold gradient on ink/night, or a single gold on photos.
  • Let the mark stand alone at small sizes.
  • Preserve the exact lockup ratios.

    • Don’t

  • Recolor, add strokes, or drop shadows onto the mark.
  • Rotate, stretch, or rearrange the lockups.
  • Set the wordmark in another font or mixed case.
  • Place the mark on low-contrast or busy backgrounds.
